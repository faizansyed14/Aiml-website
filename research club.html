Head of Reseacrh Club: Dr. G. ArunSampaul Thomas

Research clubs play a crucial role in the field of machine learning by providing a platform for students and professionals to come together and share their knowledge, ideas, and research findings. These clubs provide opportunities for members to collaborate on research projects, present their work to peers, and receive feedback and guidance from more experienced researchers.

Research clubs also help to build a community of researchers who can support and inspire each other. They can also serve as a bridge between academia and industry by connecting researchers with potential collaborators and employers.

Overall, research clubs are an essential resource for anyone working in machine learning as they provide a supportive and collaborative environment for the advancement of knowledge and innovation in the field.
