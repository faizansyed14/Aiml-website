Machine Learning Mavericks Club will organize various events and contests to engage undergraduate AI&ML students.

Machine Learning Mavericks club play a crucial role in the field of machine learning by providing a platform for students and professionals to come together and share their knowledge, ideas, and impliment them. These club provide opportunities for members to collaborate on ML projects, present their work to peers, and receive feedback and guidance from experienced faculty.

Here are some event and contest ideas for MLM club:

1. Machine Learning Hackathons: Organize hackathons that challenge students to solve realworld problems using machine learning algorithms.

2. Technical Workshops and Seminars: Organize technical workshops and seminars on machine learning topics such as data pre-processing, feature selection, model selection, and evaluation.

3. Guest Lectures: Invite guest speakers from academia and industry to speak on emerging topics in machine learning and AI.

4. Competitions: Organize competitions such as Kaggle challenges, data science challenges, and machine learning contests to encourage students to apply their knowledge and skills.

5. Projects: Encourage students to work on machine learning projects and provide mentorship and support to help them develop their ideas.

6. Research Paper Presentations: Organize paper presentation events where students can present their research findings in machine learning and AI.

These events and contests will provide an opportunity for undergraduate AI&ML students to learn, network, and apply their knowledge to real-world problems.
