Materials for AI&ML

Course materials, such as textbooks, syllabi, and handouts, are an essential part of any educational experience. They provide students with the information and resources they need to succeed in a course and understand the material being taught. Additionally, course materials can serve as a reference for students to use after the course has ended and can help to supplement lectures and other forms of instruction. Furthermore, good course materials are also important for online course as students may not have the opportunity to ask questions in person.
